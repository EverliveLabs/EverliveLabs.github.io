About Optimal Desktop

Optimal Desktop was created with a simple philosophy: to build software that feels like an extension of your mind. We believe in the power of native, lightweight applications that respect your system resources and your attention.

Optimal Desktop, was born out of a frustration with bloated, slow productivity tools. We wanted a native, lightweight workspace that lives on your desktop visible at a glance and accessible with a single keystroke to significantly enhance your productivity and workflow. It started with the basic idea of sticking a productive workspace to your desktop, just like sticky notes.

Optimal Desktop is built by Everlive Labs. We combine creativity, design, and precision to build tools for the modern creative professional.
